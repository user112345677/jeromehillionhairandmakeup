Privacy Policy

Jerome Hillion Hair And Makeup beauty salon’s website privacy policy at jeromehillionhairandmakeup.co.uk

Thank you for visiting Jerome Hillion Hair and Makeup (Jerome Hillion). This Privacy Policy governs the use of the web site and any services offered on the web site by Jerome Hillion.

This Privacy Policy has been amended and updated effective December 18, 2021.

Jerome Hillion welcomes visitors to our web site and web site has been designed to provide information and services and to permit web users to take advantage of these services. As a result, visitors will need to undergo certain confidential information is collected when they use our web site. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, the term “web site visitor” means an individual who accesses the web site by using a web browser from a web site, whether for the purpose of placing an order or receiving service or information from Jerome Hillion.

We will inform you about the reasons for collection of personal information, how the information is to be used, whether there are any legal constraints to which it is subject, who will have access to the information, and what might happen to the information if our relationship with you ends.

What information we collect

Website visitors are always sent an “acceptance message” via email, which explains that it is Jerome Hillion’s intention that they have willingly submitted their personal details to Jerome Hillion. The response, unless an email request is made, is displayed on the web page as soon as the visit is concluded.

Personal Details such as your:

Name and contact details (address, phone number and email address)

Date of birth

Employment status (details of work such as job title, company and email address)

Details of your condition, treatment, medical history or medication (details must be complete to be accepted by our Clinical Manager)

Courses and any dietary requirements you have

With your permission and preferably before completing our Check-In process, we may also keep certain information about your browsing of our site in the form of a cookie. We need information about how our site is used and collect the following types of information, such as:

Log information, such as the web pages and other data you view, use or interact with (Browser), your IP address, the page you were looking at when you visited us, the time you visited us and the time you left

Technical information, such as information about the equipment you use when browsing our website, your computer, Internet browser and what version of the browser you use

Email information, such as from which email account you use when you visit our web site and the time you visit our web site

Jerome Hillion treats your personal information with care.

You should be aware that if your device connects to the internet, it will send and receive data about your use of our web site including:

Browser details and access information, such as your IP address, time and date of visit and the page you requested. This will be sent to us in the clear when you send us emails, when you request service or send us feedback about our web site. This information may also be stored for up to two years on our web servers.

You do not have to disclose personal information to access our web site. By using our site you are accepting our Terms and Conditions. In some circumstances however, we may be legally required to provide information about our web site users. If we become subject to a legal enquiry or claim, we may also be required to disclose personal information to such third parties, including without limitation law enforcement or Government officials. Your personal information will be used for our legitimate business purposes, such as maintaining and conducting the business of our clinical hair and beauty salon or our related service companies and by conducting and improving our business. If you do not want your personal information disclosed to others you may let us know, by email, or by calling or writing us.

Law Enforcement

Information collected on our web site may also be disclosed if required or authorised by law, for example if we become a subject of a court order, administrative or other legal process or are a victim of an offence. You can let us know, in writing, if you do not want your information disclosed to law enforcement authorities.

Information we collect about your use of our web site

All web server logs, and/or other server data, are used to help keep our web site functioning properly, to be able to provide service and information to you on our web site, to track the website usage of our website visitors, and for our own business management purposes. The information included in this section is collected automatically by our web site, and the information may be used by Jerome Hillion for statistical purposes. The information collected by our web site includes:

Browser information

Your IP address and/or your device operating system

If you log into our website, your username and password

Your browsing behaviour

Statistical information such as the number of visits to our website

Technical information about how you use our website, including how you accessed the pages on our website, the URLs you have visited and how much time you spent on our web site

Location information where your location cannot be determined from your IP address

Information about how visitors use our web site is used for designing and improving our web site. We may, from time to time, send you “pop-ups” asking your opinion on a particular aspect of our web site or to obtain feedback on other aspects of our web site. Your participation in surveys and/or questionnaires that we may ask you to complete through our web site is entirely optional. We may contact you to ask your opinions on different products and services or for other reasons.

Data we collect on other websites

Our web site may collect information on websites or through services, such as Google Analytics, or by links on our web site to third party websites that use their own cookie, tracking or similar technologies. We only include links that will direct you to websites we believe are of interest to our customers. If you follow any link from Jerome Hillion’s web site to third party website, you should check the relevant privacy policy on that third party site, as Jerome Hillion is not responsible for those websites. Our web site may collect information that can be used to estimate a visitor’s age. This is done by Google Analytics which will collect information in the form of cookies.

How does the Website use Personal Information?

Jerome Hillion uses Personal Information for the following purposes:

To administer our web site, including troubleshooting, data analysis, testing, system maintenance, support, reporting and hosting.

To improve our website

To monitor and personalise content on the site (for instance, to provide you with information about products and services similar to those you have viewed on our web site, or to let you know you’re logged in when you visit a page and not if you are logged in to the web site).

To identify the most relevant content on the website for you and to sort out the most relevant results from the search engine. This will allow Jerome Hillion to customise your experience of the site.

We use Google Analytics, which is a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). Google Analytics uses “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer, to help the website analyse how users use the site. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information for the purpose of evaluating your use of the website, compiling reports on website activity for website operators and providing other services relating to website activity and internet usage. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google’s behalf. Google will not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however please note that if you do this you may not be able to use the full functionality of this website. By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

Linked websites and sources:

Jerome Hillion uses affiliate links for great discount offers to products and services on websites we link to (we use Direct Sign Ups to link products). We do not have access to or control over information that collects personal data about you when you use these sites.

Jerome Hillion does not endorse or participate in the trading of any private currency or coin. These websites do not collect or solicit personal data and there are no expectations of customer consent.

Links:

In an effort to get good quality backlinks, Jerome Hillion only allows a limited number of Links from credible websites. We have been requesting with all website owners of good quality articles, to add these links to their website. If a backlink is added from a non-credible website, and we find out that we have a bad rating at Search Engine Roundtable and Alexa. We will remove this link and your website will have a bad rating.

Jerome Hillion always uses a valid CNAME when allowing and allowing that website to be listed on our website.

Cookies:

As mentioned above, Jerome Hillion uses Google Analytics, which is a web analytics service provided by Google, Inc. (“Google”). We use Google Analytics in order to better understand how people use our site.

A “cookie” is a small text file placed on the hard drive of a visitor’s computer. The text file can be used to analyse a visitor’s activity on the site, in order to give us insight into the nature of the site, and who is visiting the site.

If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer we suggest that you do not accept or use this website.

If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer we suggest that you do not accept or use this website.

You can use the ‘customisation’ function of your browser to delete cookies from your computer. However, if you do not use the ‘customisation’ function of your browser to delete cookies, then please note that some parts of this website will not be as useful as they might otherwise be to you.
